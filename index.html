Gabriel Bravo, artistically recognized as DAKKU, is a prominent Colombian music producer born on February 16, 2006. His inspiration comes from outstanding figures in Animestep such as Svrge, Kotori, Porter Robinson, Skrillex and Virtual Riot.

DAKKU's music merges anime aesthetics with innovative sound design featuring distinctive vocal chops. At 16, he debuted with "Anyway" (360K views) and "Healing" (560K views). His breakthrough came at 17 with "Ephemeral" (1.1M views), establishing him as an evolving innovator in the Animestep landscape.
